# Finance

Finance records track animal-level income and expenses.

## Finance Fields

- Record date
- Category
- Amount
- Vendor or buyer
- Notes

## Income And Expense

Use income for sales, reimbursements, premiums, or other money received. Use expense for feed, supplies, medicine, entry fees, or other costs.

## Vet Costs

Costs entered in Vet Visits can appear beside finance records so animal-level cost is easier to review.
