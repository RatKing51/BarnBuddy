# Herds

Herds organize animals and make reporting easier.

## What Herds Are For

Use herds to group animals by:

- Species
- Show project
- Pasture or barn location
- Owner or student
- Season or breeding group

## Edit Herds

Open **Settings**, then **Herd settings** to create, rename, describe, or delete herds.

## Delete Herds

Deleting a herd does not delete its animals. Animals from the deleted herd are moved to **Unassigned**.

BarnBuddy shows a delete confirmation first, then a toast after the herd is deleted.
