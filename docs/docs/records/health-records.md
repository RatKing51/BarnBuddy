# Health Records

Health records keep care history attached to the animal that received care.

## Health Events

Use health events for:

- Illness
- Injury
- Treatment
- Checkups
- Recovery notes
- Resolved status

Recommended fields:

- Date
- Type
- Severity
- Description
- Notes

## Vaccinations

Vaccination records track:

- Vaccine name
- Date given
- Dosage
- Next due date
- Notes
- Completed status

Upcoming vaccination dates appear in care summaries and quick dates.

## Birth Details

The Health Records area can also store birth weight and birth notes for the selected animal.
